about me


Hey there, my name is Frank Chan and I am 18 years old. I live in New York City and study Computer Science at Cornell University in Ithaca. On campus I work with the Office of Academic Diversity Initiatives Mobilizers Program, which aims to build a sense of community among first generation students on campus. As part of the Communications Committee I am building a new website to serve as the face of our first generation student initiave called "Cornell First in Class" and act as a support center for first generation students. I am also a part of the Engineering World Health Project Team, which is developing a system for remotely monitoring the water quality of wells in India. I work as part of the project team's Electrical team on the web application for displaying water quality data, as well as on the Business team for rebranding and creating the team's website.

Languages: Java, C#, Javascript, Python, HTML, CSS
